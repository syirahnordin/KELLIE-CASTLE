Events

Ipoh Food Festival

The annual Ipoh Food Festival celebrates the city's variety and wonderful cuisine. Taste traditional and new foods, tour other places, and enjoy live music and cultural exhibits.

New Year Celebrations

Enjoy a thrilling countdown extravaganza with live music, dance performances, and a spectacular fireworks display. Enjoy a day of fun run, wayang pacak, and wonderful cuisine with the family.

Heritage Run

The Heritage Run is a fun way to honor our community's history. Get ready for a wonderful day of running, strolling, or jogging through historic neighborhoods and sites. Learn about our community's history and culture while enjoying its beauty.



Fun Walk & Run

Enjoy an exciting, inclusive Fun Run celebrating fitness, community, and fun. Support community groups while making memories with friends and family. Lace up your running shoes for a unique event that promotes health, pleasure, and community.

Tsukiga Kirei Matsuri

Step into a world of cosplay, anime, and gaming in our local community! Show off your costumes, compete in contests, and enjoy anime screenings. Embrace the excitement and let your passion shine in this vibrant community.

Pesta Budaya

Celebrate our diverse cultural heritage at Pesta Budaya! Enjoy captivating dance performances, engaging discussions, renowned artists, viral food, a mini zoo, and a thrilling haunted house. Embrace unity and community spirit in this unforgettable event. Join us for a vibrant celebration of our cultural tapestry.
