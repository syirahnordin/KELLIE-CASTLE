  • ▸Concubine Lane and Market Lane:
    Wander through these charming alleyways lined with heritage shophouses, art murals, and independent shops.

    ▸Perak Tong Cave Temple:
    Climb 500 steps to reach this cave temple adorned with Buddhist statues and offering panoramic views.

    ▸Birch Memorial Clock Tower:
    Immerse yourself in colonial history at this iconic landmark named after the first British Resident of Perak.

    ▸Han Chin Pet Soo Museum:
    Learn about the Hakka community and their contributions to Ipoh's development.

  • ▸Food Hawker Centers:
    Indulge in local delights like Ipoh white coffee, Hakka mee, satay, and tau fu fah at various hawker centers like Lou Wong and Nam Heong.

    ▸Old Town Coffee Shops:
    Sip on aromatic Ipoh white coffee and savor traditional pastries at iconic coffee shops like Sin Yoon Loong and Nam Seng.

    ▸Craft breweries:
    Sample locally brewed beers at The Ipoh Beer Co. or Yeast of Eden.

    ▸Bars and cafes:
    Enjoy vibrant nightlife and unique cafe experiences in areas like Ipoh Old Town and Greentown.

  • ▸Gua Tempurung:
    Embark on a guided adventure through this cave complex, filled with fascinating rock formations and underground streams.

    ▸Gunung Lang Recreational Park:
    Hike through lush rainforests and scenic trails, offering breathtaking views of the city.

    ▸Lost World of Tambun:
    Enjoy water rides, theme park attractions, and natural hot springs at this popular destination.

    ▸Belum Rainforest:
    Take a day trip from Ipoh to explore this UNESCO World Heritage Site, teeming with diverse flora and fauna.

  • ▸Mural Hunting:
    Spot hidden street art depicting historical figures, local folklore, and contemporary themes throughout the city.

    ▸Museum Hopping:
    Visit the Perak Museum for a comprehensive overview of the state's history and culture, or discover more specific interests at museums like the Yasmin Ahmad Museum and the Ho Yan Hor Museum.

    ▸Birch Memorial Clock Tower:
    Immerse yourself in colonial history at this iconic landmark named after the first British Resident of Perak.

    ▸Night Markets:
    Browse for souvenirs, local crafts, and delicious street food at the vibrant Ipoh Night Market or the Pasar Karat Ipoh.
